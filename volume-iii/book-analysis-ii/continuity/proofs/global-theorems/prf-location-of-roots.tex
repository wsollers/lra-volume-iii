\begin{theorem*}[Location of Roots Theorem]
Let $f : [a,b] \to \mathbb{R}$ be continuous on $[a,b]$. If $f(a) < 0 < f(b)$ or $f(a) > 0 > f(b)$, then there exists $c \in (a,b)$ such that $f(c) = 0$.
\end{theorem*}
