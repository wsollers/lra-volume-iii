\begin{corollary*}[Finite Sum Rule]
If $f_1, f_2, \dots, f_n$ are functions on an interval $I$ to $\mathbb{R}$ that are differentiable at $c \in I$, then:
\[ \left( \sum_{i=1}^{n} f_i \right)'(c) = \sum_{i=1}^{n} f_i'(c) \]
\end{corollary*}
