\begin{corollary*}[Continuity Criterion for Monotone Functions]
Let $f : I \to \mathbb{R}$ be monotone increasing and let $c \in I$ be an interior point. Then $f$ is continuous at $c$ if and only if $f(c^{-}) = f(c^{+})$.
\end{corollary*}
