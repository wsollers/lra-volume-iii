\begin{corollary*}[Nested Intervals with Vanishing Length Have Equal Endpoint Limits]
Let $\{[a_n,b_n]\}_{n\in\mathbb N}$ be a nested sequence of nonempty closed
bounded intervals. If $\lim_{n\to\infty}(b_n-a_n)=0$, then $(a_n)$ and
$(b_n)$ converge and $\lim_{n\to\infty}a_n=\lim_{n\to\infty}b_n$.
\end{corollary*}
