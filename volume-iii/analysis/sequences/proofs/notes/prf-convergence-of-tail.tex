\begin{theorem*}[Convergence of a Tail]
Let $(x_n) \subseteq \mathbb{R}$ and let $m \in \mathbb{N}$. The
$m$-tail $(x_{m+n})_{n\in\mathbb{N}}$ converges if and only if $(x_n)$
converges, and the limits agree.
\end{theorem*}
