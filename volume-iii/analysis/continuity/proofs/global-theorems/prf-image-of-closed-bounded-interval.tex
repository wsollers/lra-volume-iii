\begin{theorem*}[Image of a Closed Bounded Interval Is a Closed Bounded Interval]
Let $f : [a,b] \to \mathbb{R}$ be continuous on the closed bounded interval $[a,b]$. Then $f([a,b]) = [m, M]$, where $m = \inf f([a,b])$ and $M = \sup f([a,b])$.
\end{theorem*}
