\begin{corollary*}[Unique Point in Nested Intervals Is Endpoint Limit]
Let $\{[a_n,b_n]\}_{n\in\mathbb N}$ be a nested sequence of nonempty closed
bounded intervals. If $\lim_{n\to\infty}(b_n-a_n)=0$ and
$\bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty}[a_n,b_n]=\{x\}$, then
$\lim_{n\to\infty}a_n=x$ and $\lim_{n\to\infty}b_n=x$.
\end{corollary*}
