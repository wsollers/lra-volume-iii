\section*{Exercises}
\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{Exercises}

The following exercises are grouped by section. Each group contains five
problems of graduated difficulty. For each exercise, write your solution
in the Work Area. Proofs should cite the specific definition, proposition,
or theorem being applied. Computations should show each step explicitly.
