\hyperref[thm:absolute-error-sum]{Sum rule} & Absolute errors add under addition \\
\hyperref[thm:absolute-error-product]{Product rule} & Product errors have two linear terms and one second-order term \\
\hyperref[thm:absolute-error-quotient]{Quotient rule} & Division amplifies error near small denominators \\
\hyperref[prop:relative-error-product]{Relative product rule} & Relative product errors are approximately additive \\
\hyperref[prop:relative-error-quotient]{Relative quotient rule} & Relative quotient errors are amplified by denominator uncertainty \\
