\begin{theorem*}[Leibniz Rule]
Let \(I\subseteq\mathbb{R}\) be an interval, let \(x\in I\), and suppose
that \(f,g:I\to\mathbb{R}\) are \(n\)-times differentiable on a
neighbourhood of \(x\). Then
\[
  (fg)^{(n)}(x)
  =
  \sum_{k=0}^{n}\binom{n}{k}f^{(k)}(x)g^{(n-k)}(x).
\]
\end{theorem*}
