Let $f$ be twice differentiable in a neighbourhood of $c$, and suppose $f'(c)=0$. If $f''(c)>0$, then $f$ has a strict relative minimum at $c$. If $f''(c)<0$, then $f$ has a strict relative maximum at $c$.
\end{theorem*}
