\begin{corollary*}[Nested Intervals with Vanishing Length Have Unique Point]
Let $\{[a_n,b_n]\}_{n\in\mathbb N}$ be a nested sequence of nonempty closed
bounded intervals. If $\lim_{n\to\infty}(b_n-a_n)=0$, then there exists a
unique $x\in\mathbb R$ such that
$\bigcap_{n=1}^{\infty}[a_n,b_n]=\{x\}$.
\end{corollary*}
